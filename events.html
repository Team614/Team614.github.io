Sling Shot

Hayfield Robotics Team 614 presents, "Slingshot" movie screening at Hayfield Secondary School, Rebecca S. Wilburn Auditorium on Friday, February 27 from 5:00PM -- 7:30. The film focuses on FIRST founder, noted inventor Dean Kamen, his fascinating life, and his 15-year quest to solve the world's water crisis. SlingShot is both an inspirational character study and a look at Kamen's vapor compression distiller from its earliest development through recent trials in rural Ghana and beyond. Please join us for a Hayfield Robotics fundraiser and watch a robotic demonstration, including our 2015 robot reveal. Tickets are $10 each and sold online at https://www.tugg.com/events/12948 .There will be light refreshments served. Hope to see you there!
